My Collection Favorites

Soldier 76

One of my favorites is Soldier 76. One of my favorite characters to play in Overwatch, this pop is exclusive to GameStop. I love the design and being one of my most played characters it was a pop that I really wanted to add to my collection. After it released the demand for it has increased and the price increased from its retail value at $11.99 to around $50.00.

Bombshell Harley Quinn - Think Geek Exclusive

I’m a Harley Quinn fan so tend to buy any Harley Quinn pop that I see and don’t own. This was a must have as the Bombshell look of the pop reminds me of the game series Fallout which I love. This particular pop is a ThinkGeek exclusive, a store with few locations. This pop was also distributed through GameStop’s, unfortunately for collectors most stores only received enough for the people who had preorder her. Thus her value remains a bit high at around $55.00.

Daedric Warrior

Unlike the other two, my Daedric Warrior pop is not worth more than its retail value. Even so it remains one of my favorite pops of all time. The detail of the pop is amazing, better than many of Funko’s products.

My Collection

poppriceguide.com

    My current collection not including my customs sits at 78 pops. Funko has many lines that include, Books, Disney, Football, Harry Potter, Magic, Marvel, Movies, Television, WWE, and a lot more. They have collectibles for everyone. Due to budget constraints I collect only the lines that I really like. I collect pop from the Animation line (cartoons or anime), Games line (anything gaming related falls in this category), and the Heroes line.
